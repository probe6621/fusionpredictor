Important Product Notes

FusionPredict

Timeframe Behavior: Lower-timeframe projections behave with near real-time responsiveness (depending on volatility), while higher intervals offer great context for directional bias and price approximations. For example, a 1-minute timeframe equals approximately 1 minute in real-time, but full timeframe moves on higher intervals take proportionally longer to complete.

Volatility Considerations: FusionPredict is most precise in calm or moderate volatility. During high-volatility events, price may seek liquidity first before reverting toward the projected level—a natural behavior consistent with advanced gap-reversion dynamics. The targets remain valid; only the order of events can shift during extreme movement.

Pure Alignment Advantage: When all timeframes align in one direction, entries are significantly cleaner without retracement whipping back to your entry point. This pure directional alignment provides the highest-probability setups.

Trending Market Caveat: In strongly trending markets, targets may take longer to reach as momentum can extend beyond normal reversion ranges. Use discretion and consider the broader trend context when taking signals during extreme directional moves.
