How It Works

The science behind the energy depletion gauge

The Reversion Meter doesn't predict direction — it confirms timing. When the gauge shows high exhaustion (red zones) while FusionPredict signals alignment, you have maximum confidence that the market has depleted its energy and is ready to revert.

Low gauge readings (green zones) mean momentum is still strong — the market has energy to continue. Wait for exhaustion before taking reversion trades.

Volume Exhaustion

Dramatic volume decline signals retail exhaustion and potential reversion opportunities.

ATR Percentile

Measures if recent volatility is extreme relative to historical ranges.

Momentum Flatness

Detects when directional momentum is decelerating and losing strength.

Directional Pressure

Tracks the balance of buying vs selling pressure for exhaustion confirmation.

Key Insight: Gap reversion predictions only work when market energy is depleted. During strong trends or liquidity-seeking moves, predictions fail because the market has energy to continue. The Reversion Meter tells you when conditions are right.
