Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my indicators after purchase?

Once payment completes, our system automatically grants TradingView access to the username you entered at checkout. Find your indicators in TradingView under: Indicators → Invite-Only Scripts

I can't find my indicators on TradingView

Common reasons include incorrect TradingView username, using your email instead of username, or typos with spaces/capitals. Double-check your username and contact us if the issue persists.

Can I change my TradingView username?

Yes — simply email us your old and new username and we'll update your access within 24 hours.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Visit your billing portal at fusionpredictor.com/my-account.html. Your access remains active until the end of your billing period.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes — you can change your plan anytime through the billing portal. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do the indicators work on all markets?

Yes — FusionPredictor indicators work on crypto, forex, futures, indices, and stocks. Any asset available on TradingView is supported.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 14 days for a full refund. After access is granted, refunds are only issued for duplicate charges, technical issues, or charges after cancellation. Full policy →

Do I need TradingView Pro?

No — FusionPredictor works on all TradingView account levels including free. However, Pro/Premium is recommended for more alerts and multi-chart layouts.
