Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my indicators after purchase?

After completing your purchase, your TradingView username is automatically granted access to your indicators. This usually happens within 5-10 minutes. Search for "FusionPredict" (or other indicator names) in TradingView's indicator search, and you should see them with an "Invite-only" label.

I can't find my indicators on TradingView

Make sure you're searching in TradingView's Indicators panel (not Scripts). If you still can't find them, double-check that the TradingView username you provided during checkout matches your actual TradingView account. Contact support with your email and username for assistance.

Can I change my TradingView username?

Yes! Contact support with your current email, old TradingView username, and new TradingView username. We'll update your access within 24 hours.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Email [email protected] with your account email and we'll process your cancellation. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! Contact support to change your plan. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

Do indicators work on all markets?

Yes! FusionPredictor indicators work on any market available on TradingView—stocks, crypto, forex, futures, and more. They're optimized for timeframes from 1-minute to daily charts.

What's the refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, contact support for a full refund—no questions asked.

Do I need TradingView Pro?

The free TradingView plan works, but has limitations on alerts. We recommend TradingView Pro+ or higher if you want to use multiple alerts across different assets.
