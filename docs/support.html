Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my indicators after purchase?

Once your payment is completed, our system automatically grants TradingView access to the username you entered at checkout.

You will find your indicators in TradingView under:
Indicators → Invite-Only Scripts

I can't find my indicators on TradingView

Common reasons include:

• Incorrect TradingView username entered
• You used your email instead of your username
• Username contains spaces or capitals

If unsure, email us at support@fusionpredictor.com.

Can I change my TradingView username?

Yes — simply email us your old and new username and we will update your access.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Visit your billing portal:
fusionpredictor.com/my-account.html

Your access remains active until the end of your billing period.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes — you may change your plan anytime through your billing portal.

Do the indicators work on all markets?

Yes — they work on crypto, forex, futures, indices, and stocks.

What is the refund policy?

Because access to our proprietary indicators is delivered instantly, we do not offer refunds once access has been granted.

Refunds are only issued for:
• Duplicate charges
• Technical issues preventing access
• Charges after cancellation

Full policy:
fusionpredictor.com/refunds.html

Do I need TradingView Pro?

No — FusionPredictor works on all account levels.

Pro/Premium is recommended for multi-chart, multi-timeframe setups.
