FusionPredict™ MTF Projection Engine

FusionPredict’s proprietary multi-timeframe (MTF) engine is purpose-built for lower-timeframe scalpers and intraday traders who need forward-looking, high-confidence precision levels. The system generates projected price targets before they complete — allowing traders to anticipate where price is most likely to move next with remarkable accuracy.

How It Works

The FusionPredict engine combines displacement, volatility, flow signatures, and incoming-order dynamics to forecast future price levels across 1m–1h intervals. These projections update in near real-time, reacting instantly to volatility changes and directional shifts.

Projection Intervals

The engine outputs six simultaneous MTF projections:

  • 5m — the gold standard for scalpers
  • 10m — medium-flow context
  • 15m — directional bias anchor
  • 30m — structural slope confirmation
  • 1h — macro pressure guide

Accuracy

Backtesting across stable market regimes has shown up to 97% projection accuracy on the 5-minute interval. Higher timeframes provide broader trend alignment while lower intervals deliver precision entries and exit points.

Volatility Behavior

FusionPredict is most precise in calm or moderate conditions. During high-volatility events, price may temporarily sweep liquidity before reverting to its projected level — a natural part of advanced gap-reversion behavior.
