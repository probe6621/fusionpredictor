How FusionPredict Generates Targets

A simplified look at the prediction engine's process

1

Market Sentiment Analysis

Multiple market indicators are analyzed across each timeframe. Their values are normalized and combined into a composite representing market sentiment.

2

Displacement Detection

The difference between real-time readings and smoothed averages reveals how far price has displaced from equilibrium. Larger gaps signal stronger reversion potential.

3

Target Projection

Current price is adjusted by the calculated displacement to produce a concrete price target. Each timeframe generates its own target independently.

4

MTF Alignment Check

When all timeframe targets align on the same side of price (all above or all below), a high-conviction signal fires. This is the "everything agrees" moment traders wait for.
