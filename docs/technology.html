How FusionPredict Generates Targets

A simplified look at the prediction engine's process

1

Oscillator Analysis

Five oscillators (RSI, Stochastic, MFI, CMF, CCI) are analyzed across each timeframe. Their values are normalized and combined into a "Ribbon Average" representing market sentiment.

2

Gap Calculation

The difference between the Ribbon Average and a smoothed Confluence Meter reveals how far price has displaced from equilibrium. Larger gaps = stronger reversion signals.

3

Target Projection

Current price is adjusted by the gap multiplied by visible range to produce a concrete price target. Each timeframe generates its own target independently.

4

MTF Alignment Check

When all timeframe targets align on the same side of price (all above or all below), a high-conviction signal fires. This is the "everything agrees" moment traders wait for.
